About Saveetha Engineering College

Saveetha Engineering College (SEC), Chennai, established in 2001, is a prestigious institution dedicated to providing high-quality technical education. Affiliated with Anna University, SEC is an ISO 9001:2015 certified institution committed to fostering academic excellence and holistic development of students.

Our sprawling campus is equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure, including modern laboratories, a well-stocked library, and extensive sports facilities, creating an environment conducive to learning and innovation.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a leading institution of higher learning by imparting knowledge, fostering innovation, and promoting research to create socially responsible engineers and technologists.

Mission

To achieve excellence in engineering education through effective teaching, industry collaboration, and a commitment to lifelong learning, preparing students for global challenges.

Key Departments

Computer Science and Engineering

Focuses on software development, data science, AI, and cybersecurity.

Electronics and Communication Engineering

Covers VLSI design, embedded systems, and communication networks.

Mechanical Engineering

Emphasizes design, manufacturing, thermal sciences, and robotics.

Information Technology

Deals with software engineering, cloud computing, and network management.

Civil Engineering

Involves structural engineering, transportation, and environmental engineering.

Biomedical Engineering

Integrates engineering principles with medical sciences for healthcare innovation.
